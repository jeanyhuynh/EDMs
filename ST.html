—

Why Attend

Cybersecurity attacks are becoming more complicated and it is spreading widely globally. It is therefore important to safeguard the organisation from such attacks and in order to do so successfully, one must understand how to identify, analyse, respond and investigate cyber-attacks.

 

By attending this course, one will be able to understand the protection of SCADA networks within a realistic control system environment. Course participants will also have hands-on practice on the various tools to handle potential attacks on the control systems.

Learning Outcomes

Participants will learn how cyber-attacks are launched, how they work, and mitigation strategies. Through a comparative analysis of IT and control system architecture, security vulnerabilities and mitigation strategies.

Course Topics

  • Cyber Physical System Overview

  • IT & OT Networks Discovery

  • Cyber Physical System and Network Attacks and Exploits

  • Network Defence and Incident Response

Prerequisites

Basic understanding of industrial control system, cyber physical system or operational technology recommended but not required.

Who Should Attend

  • Corporate or Industrial Asset Owners/Vendors/Integrators

  • IT Professionals/Engineers

  • Information Security Managers and Executives

  • Solution Architects

  • SCADA Engineers

Cybersecurity Forensic Investigator (CSFI) - 4 Days

5 to 8 August 2019 | Registration closes: 26 July 2019

14 to 17  October 2019 | Registration closes: 13 September 2019

Why Attend

Do you want to gain better understanding of cyber forensics and acquire investigation skillsets? The Cybersecurity Forensics Investigator course focuses on imparting you with cyber forensics concepts and processes including acquisition of forensics evidences, preservation and analysis of the gathered evidences, and presentation of the findings.

 

The training also encompasses the conduct of forensics investigation on simulated real-world cyber incidents in an emulated enterprise network environment to instill applicable skillsets and enhances knowledge retention. CSFI course provides you with a robust cyber forensics investigation training experience.

Learning Outcomes

  • Understand the entire cyber forensics investigation processes and procedures

  • Understand functionality of various forensic tools and its use during investigation

  • Develop practical skillsets in forensics investigation from acquisition, preservation, analysis to reporting

  • Recommend preventive measures against future cyber incidents

Course Objectives

  • Gain knowledge about computer based crime.

  • Develop skills to identify and collect evidence from computer based operating system.

Prerequisites

This course is intended for those with knowledge and hands-on experience on Personal Computers and Network Fundamental, such as CompTIA A+ and Network+ certifications or the equivalent. The intended learners also expected to be familiar with the command line interfaces of the 2 popular OS, Windows, and Linux.

Who Should Attend

  • Security Analysts

  • IT Professionals / Engineers

  • Information Security Managers and Executives

  • Project Managers, Risk Managers and Compliance Managers
