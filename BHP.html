Health and Safety

Health tip of the week – Helping you cope more effectively with stress

When you foresee a demanding time coming up make sure you are in the best position possible by maintaining your energy levels.

This includes eating healthy. A balanced meal of whole grains, lean protein, fibre-rich fruits, vegetables and healthy fats will give you sustained physical energy.

In addition to healthy eating, consider:

  • Physical energy – prioritise your sleep; take breaks

  • Emotional energy –make sure that you deal with negative emotions; show appreciation; make real connections with your loved ones.

  • Mental energy – make sure you prioritise and plan, allocate enough time / resources for tasks, and reduce interruptions.

  • Spiritual energy (or your sense of self ) - know your values and goals and live your life in accordance with them. Say no to things that are in conflict with your values and goals.

These elements are collectively known as the “Energy Project”. Click here to learn more on this.
